About Me - Yolanda Suda-Moore


A Mom/Wife with a passion for helping others the "natural" way! Sharing Essential Oils with family and friends to help their Mind Body & Soul.


One of my favorite essential oil is what I call "liquid sunshine"! Living in western Washington, on those rainy, windy days, I especially enjoy diffusing my lemon oil and adding it to my water throughout the day. Did you know that the "citrus" oils are also the "happy" oils?

Another happy oil, Orange, combined with the Thieves blend are great to diffuse together! WHY? Well not only does the combination of Orange & Thieves (Cinnamon, Clove, Lemon, Eucalyptus, Rosemary) smell AMAZING! It’s cleaning the air and helping anyone that comes into my house!

THIEVES is a blend of essential oils that are known for being highly supportive to the immune system. This is one of the reasons I LOVE the Young Living Thieves cleaner for my house!!! Yes you can mix oils and create your own cleaner but why not save those oils to use on yourself and others or to make other DIY things! There is an entire "THIEVES" line of products already expertly prepared by Young Living for your home.

The Ningxia Wolfberry is also a key ingredient in Young Living’s Ningxia Red and is an amazing Super Food! It is known to be the richest whole-food source of natural vitamin B1 or Thiamin, which is essential for proper energy production, carbohydrate metabolism, and thyroid function! Ningxia Wolfberry legends and records of ancient Chinese Medicine reach back 5,000 years!!! And YES, kids can have these too!
