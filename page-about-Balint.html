About Me - Bálint Bátka


I grew up in Budapest, Hungary and am currently living and working in Las Vegas after earning a Bachelor of Science in Hospitality Management; Concentration in Gaming Management at UNLV. I attended UNLV on an athletic scholarship for the Mens Swim Team.


I was a competitive swimmer most of my life, starting at the age of four. Training hard and being competitive has always been a way of life for me and I found success on the international and collegiate level. My college swimming accomplishments include:
• Academic All Conference all four years
• Western Athletic Conference 1st Team-200 Fly
• Mountain Pacific Sports Federation Champion-200 Fly
• 2013 NCAA All American


I enjoy spending time with my friends and family, watching Netflix, working out, and staying active.

At the YL farm in France.


I learned about Young Living from my college roommate. I was first intrigued by Ningxia Red and used it as an all natural supplement for added support for my immune system. My favorite essential oil blend is Deep Relief especially after a great workout.


My family in Budpest enjoys Young Living essential oils too!
• Lavender is my mother’s favorite
• My father adds a drop of Lemon to his water every morning
• My sister’s favorite is Purification


Young Living products are high quality and very versatile. Contact myself or my mother for assistance in learning more.
