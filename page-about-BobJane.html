About Us - Bob & Jane Roe

Bob is a retired Boeing Executive and Jane is a retired swim coach and “Take Shape For Life” Health Coach. We have three adult children and two granddaughters. We both grew up in Washington state but we were drawn to the majestic beauty of Montana in the spring of 2013. We finished building our home in Montana in the fall of 2016. We enjoy fly fishing, hiking, swimming, crafting, and travel.




Our Health Journey

Jane was adopted as a baby and started off her life without the immune supportive benefits of breast milk. Unfortunately, the antibiotic use began within her first couple of months of life; so much so that her teeth are permanently discolored. She even had surgery at the age of 4 because of one chronic condition. She lived the first 48 years of her life in a chronic cycle of antibiotic use! None of the issues were really very serious, so she was otherwise considered healthy and enjoyed competitive swimming and track. Bob grew up as a healthy athlete in a variety of sports, including swimming, basketball, baseball, and golf.

What we do every day makes a difference!

As adults working many hours we both let our daily habits effect our health. In December of 2008, Bob had a heart attack and Jane was in the obese range. It became clear to us that the unhealthy effects of processed food and poor choices had taken it’s toll. We began to make some intentional lifestyle changes. Bob quit smoking, we were reading labels, and being intentional about what we were eating. Over the next year and a half, Bob had lost 30 lbs and Jane had lost over 50 lbs. This gave us a new lease on life, enabled a more active lifestyle, and purpose to help others with healthy weight management.


Our first experience with Young Living Essential Oils began with Frankincense and Myrrh at a 2010 Christmas shopping event. As we began to learn more about how effective therapeutic grade essential oils could support the body systems, our awareness of the toxins found in typical household and personal care products increased. This led us to systematically remove as many toxins as we could from our home (toxic or synthetic cleaners, air fresheners, personal care products, low quality supplements, over the counter remedies, etc) and replaced them with Young Living’s all-natural products. We simply transferred purchases from the grocery store to our Young Living online order.

We believe that reducing toxins in our homes and our bodies help to support our body systems.


We encourage you to examine your daily choices and discuss with us ways in which you too can achieve success in making positive lifestyle changes! Whether you have a desire to create optimal health for yourself, a safer home for your family, or an interest in joining the Healthy Connections Team, we’re here to help!
